About The Heuheu


theheuheu.com offers free, simple online tools you can use instantly—no accounts, no sign-ups, no friction.

The Heuheu is a digital junk drawer creature, collecting small, practical tools you can reach for when you need to get something done quickly.

This site is built on the idea that everyday tools should remain accessible. The tools here are free by design, with the goal of keeping them that way.

Each tool is designed to be easily bookmarked, ready whenever you need it.

Your privacy matters. Processing happens locally in your browser, and your files are not uploaded to or stored on a server.

We keep things minimal, reliable, and focused so you can finish your task and move on with your day.
